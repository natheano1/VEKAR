Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who we are

Gold Coast DJ Hire is a sole trader business providing professional DJ services for weddings, corporate events, private events and ceremonies across the Gold Coast regions of Queensland, Australia.

For any privacy related enquiries, please contact us at: hello@goldcoastdjhire.com.au

2. What personal information we collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you make an enquiry through our website contact form. This may include:

  • Your full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Event type and event date
  • Any additional details you provide in your message

We also automatically collect certain non-personal technical information when you visit our website, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring website. This is collected via Google Analytics 4 (GA4) and Google Tag Manager (GTM) using cookies.

3. How we collect your information

We collect personal information in the following ways:

  • Directly from you when you submit our website contact form
  • When you email us directly at hello@goldcoastdjhire.com.au
  • Automatically through cookies and analytics tools when you browse our website

6. How we store and protect your information

Your personal information is stored securely. Enquiry data submitted through our contact form is processed via Formspree and delivered to our business email. We take reasonable steps to protect your information from misuse, loss, unauthorised access, modification or disclosure.

7. Disclosure of your information

We do not sell, trade or rent your personal information to third parties. We may disclose your information to the following trusted service providers:

  • Google (Analytics and Tag Manager)
  • Formspree (contact form processing)
  • Zoho Mail (email services)
  • GitHub Pages (website hosting)

8. Your rights under Australian Privacy Law

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent for us to use your information
  • Lodge a complaint about how we handle your personal information

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.

9. Links to third party websites

Our website may contain links to third party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted on this page with a revised date.

11. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@goldcoastdjhire.com.au
  • Website: goldcoastdjhire.com.au
  • Location: Gold Coast, Queensland, Australia
