The Process

How it works

Step 1

Initial Chat

Tell us about your event — the occasion, the vibe you're after, the venue, and your guest profile.

Step 2

Tailored Quote

We provide a custom quote based on your event duration, location, equipment needs and any special requests.

Step 3

Booking Deposit

A deposit secures your date. We'll send through a simple event brief for you to complete.

Step 4

Music Planning

We collaborate on your playlist — genres, eras, must-play tracks and anything you'd like avoided.

Step 5

Pre-Event Confirmation

A week out we reconfirm all details — arrival time, setup, any last-minute changes to the run sheet.

Step 6

Setup & Soundcheck

We arrive early to set up and test all equipment so everything is party-ready before your guests arrive.

Step 7

The Event

We deliver a high-energy, crowd-reading performance that keeps the vibe exactly where you want it all night long.
