What we offer

We specialise in creating the perfect musical atmosphere for corporate events of all sizes. Whether it's a product launch, end-of-year gala, awards night, or team celebration, we understand the importance of professionalism and precision. The music we choose reflects your brand and elevates your event from ordinary to unforgettable.

The Process

How it works

Step 1

Initial Consultation

We discuss your event goals, company culture, preferred music style and any specific requirements. This helps us understand exactly what you need.

Step 2

Proposal & Quote

We provide a tailored proposal outlining our services, equipment and pricing — typically within 24–48 hours of your enquiry.

Step 3

Event Confirmation

Once you're happy, we lock in your date with a booking deposit and send through a formal event brief for your records.

Step 4

Pre-Event Planning

2–4 weeks before your event we confirm all logistics — venue access, setup times, equipment requirements and the run sheet.

Step 5

Day of Event Setup

We arrive 1–2 hours early to set up and soundcheck all equipment, ensuring everything is perfect before your guests arrive.

Step 6

Performance

We deliver a polished, professional set tailored specifically to your event — reading the room and adjusting energy levels as the evening progresses.

Step 7

Pack Down

After your event we pack down quietly and efficiently, leaving the venue as we found it.
