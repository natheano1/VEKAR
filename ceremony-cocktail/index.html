Ceremony & Cocktail

Ceremony & Cocktail Hour

Overview

What we offer

The ceremony and cocktail hour set the entire tone for your event. Subtle, carefully chosen music creates an atmosphere of warmth and anticipation — welcoming guests, marking special moments, and bridging the gap beautifully between the formal ceremony and the celebration to follow. We handle this transitional music with the elegance it deserves.

The Process

How it works

Step 1

Enquiry & Availability

We check availability for your date and discuss the specific requirements of your ceremony and cocktail hour.

Step 2

Music Consultation

We discuss the mood and style you're after — from acoustic and ambient to light jazz or modern acoustic covers.

Step 3

Song Selection

We work through your ceremony processional, signing music, recessional and cocktail hour playlist together.

Step 4

Venue Walkthrough

Where possible, we visit or virtually review the venue layout to plan speaker placement and cable runs.

Step 5

Day of Setup

We arrive early to set up discreetly, ensuring equipment is ready and sound levels are perfect before guests arrive.

Step 6

Ceremony Performance

Live or curated music for the full ceremony — processional, signing, and recessional — timed perfectly to your cues.

Step 7

Cocktail Hour

Smooth, elegant background music during drinks and canapés, setting the mood perfectly for the celebration ahead.
